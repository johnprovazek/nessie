About:

Nessie the Great is a band from San Jose, California. They play that sweet reggae rock music you've heard so much about. Juan Carlos Sandoval and Carson Lewis started playing music together in 2014 but were always deprived of a drummer. Later on in 2016, Carson saw this guy at school wearing the same sublime shirt that he had and decided to randomly go up to him asking him if he played drums. It turned out that this guy (Nic Corona) actually did play drums and a week later they all started jamming together. This was the foundation of what soon after was named "Nessie the Great". Our primary goals with this band is to make people groove.

Band Members:

Carson Lewis

Plays the guitar and is known to slay on the mic. He is also an accomplished basoon player and in his free time loves building minature sailboats for the community.

Juan-Carlos Sandoval

Spiritual leader and group shamen. When he's not taking sips from his trusty canteen he loves to "Slappa da bass". Ask him about his marble collection.

Nic Corona

Born with a drum stick in each one of his hands and has been banging on drums ever since. When he's not filming Ghost Rider 5 he enjoys taking long walks on the beach and flying kites.
